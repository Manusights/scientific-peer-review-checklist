# Editorial Triage Checklist

Use this before full external review.

## Fit and scope
- Manuscript matches journal scope
- Claim magnitude aligns with target audience

## Integrity and ethics
- IRB/ethics approval stated where relevant
- Consent statements present if required
- Data/image manipulation concerns screened

## Methodological baseline
- Sample and controls appear adequate
- Core methods are interpretable and reproducible

## Decision
- Send to review
- Request technical clarifications first
- Desk reject with rationale

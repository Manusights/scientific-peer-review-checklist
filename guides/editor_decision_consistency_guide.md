# Editor Decision Consistency Guide

Editors need consistent decision standards across manuscripts and reviewers.

## Decision consistency framework
Use three aligned inputs:
1. manuscript claim strength
2. evidence quality
3. review consensus on critical validity issues

## Decision buckets
- Send to review
- Major revision
- Minor revision
- Reject

Each decision should include one short rationale tied to evidence quality.

## Handling conflicting reviews
When reviewers disagree:
- isolate the disagreement dimension
- request targeted clarification or specialist input
- avoid averaging opinions without methodological resolution

## Decision memo structure
- manuscript summary in 2 lines
- top 2 validity issues
- current decision and threshold for reversal

# Editor Decision Language

## Desk reject examples
- "The manuscript is outside the scope and audience priorities of this journal."
- "The reported advance does not meet the novelty threshold expected for this venue."

## Send to review example
- "The question is relevant and potentially strong, pending external expert assessment of methodological rigor."

## Major revision example
- "The reviewers identified substantial evidence gaps that must be addressed before this work can be considered further."

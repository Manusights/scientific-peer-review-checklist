# Major Revision Framework

Use this when the manuscript has promise but key evidence is missing.

## Structure for the review
1. One-paragraph summary of what the paper tries to show
2. Three to five major issues, ranked by impact on validity
3. Concrete path for each issue
4. Clear decision threshold for acceptance

## Good major issue format
- Issue: The central mechanism claim is not yet supported.
- Why it matters: This claim drives the title and abstract.
- Needed evidence: Add experiment X or tone down claims accordingly.

## Decision line
"I recommend major revision. Acceptance requires direct evidence for claims A and B."

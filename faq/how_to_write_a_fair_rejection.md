# How to Write a Fair Rejection

A fair rejection:
- Names the core evidence gap
- Avoids personal language
- Gives one clear threshold that was not met


## Quick checklist
- Is the issue tied to validity or interpretation?
- Is the requested change concrete and testable?
- Is review tone neutral and specific?

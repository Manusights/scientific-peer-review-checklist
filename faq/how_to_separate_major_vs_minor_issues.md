# How to Separate Major vs Minor Issues

Major issues change validity or interpretation.
Minor issues improve clarity or presentation.

If fixing the issue could change the conclusion, it is major.


## Quick checklist
- Is the issue tied to validity or interpretation?
- Is the requested change concrete and testable?
- Is review tone neutral and specific?

# How to Review When Methods Are Unclear

Request clarifications in specific terms:
- What is missing
- Why it prevents evaluation
- What exact detail is needed


## Quick checklist
- Is the issue tied to validity or interpretation?
- Is the requested change concrete and testable?
- Is review tone neutral and specific?

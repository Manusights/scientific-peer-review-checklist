# How to Check Figure-Claim Alignment

For each main claim, find the exact supporting figure and panel.

If you cannot map claim to figure in under one minute, the manuscript needs revision.


## Quick checklist
- Is the issue tied to validity or interpretation?
- Is the requested change concrete and testable?
- Is review tone neutral and specific?

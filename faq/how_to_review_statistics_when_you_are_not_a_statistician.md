# How to Review Statistics When You Are Not a Statistician

Focus on transparency first:
- Are tests named clearly?
- Are assumptions discussed?
- Are effect sizes and uncertainty shown?

Escalate to specialist review when needed.


## Quick checklist
- Is the issue tied to validity or interpretation?
- Is the requested change concrete and testable?
- Is review tone neutral and specific?

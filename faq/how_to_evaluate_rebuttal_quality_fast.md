# How to Evaluate Rebuttal Quality Fast

Check three things:
- Did authors answer each major concern directly?
- Did they add real evidence, not only wording changes?
- Did they revise claims where evidence is still limited?


## Quick checklist
- Is the issue tied to validity or interpretation?
- Is the requested change concrete and testable?
- Is review tone neutral and specific?
